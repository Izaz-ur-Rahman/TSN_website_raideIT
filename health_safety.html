Health and Safety Policy

At TSN (Telecommunication Services Network), our commitment is to ensure a healthy and injury-free workplace for all workers and visitors. We actively encourage daily participation by everyone in every job to achieve health and safety excellence.

  • Complete site induction and first aid training
  • Wear appropriate PPE
  • Be on-site for a valid reason
  • Be competent and fit to carry out the work
  • Comply with the Safety Manual and carry a Health and Safety Competence Passbook recording training

Training and Competence

At TSN, we believe that training is a crucial aspect of maintaining a safe working environment. Our comprehensive training programs ensure that all employees are equipped with the necessary skills and knowledge to perform their duties safely and effectively.

  • Regular safety training sessions
  • Competence assessments and certifications
  • Emergency response drills
  • Continuous development and updates on safety procedures

Hazard Analysis and Equipment Safety

Our approach to hazard analysis and equipment safety involves rigorous assessments and adherence to strict safety protocols. We continuously evaluate potential risks and implement measures to mitigate them.

  • Regular hazard assessments
  • Safety equipment inspections
  • Maintenance and safety checks
  • Compliance with industry standards
