Engineer Syed Amjad Iqbal

President & CEO

Engineer Syed's professional journey is a testament to his ambition and drive. He came to New Zealand under the Skilled Migrant category from Sweden in 2014, and after a year of lecturing in Project Management and Telecommunication, he decided to take a bold step into entrepreneurship. His company, TSN, quickly expanded from Auckland to Waikato and the South Island, with over 30 teams across the country, showcasing his rapid success as an entrepreneur.

He worked as a Telecommunication Engineer in Pakistan, Norway, and New Zealand, where he designed Telecom networks, installed Telecommunication equipment, and managed projects and contracts.

Engr. Syed has very strong team-building, stakeholder management, project management, and contract management skills and is responsible for the development and execution of commercial strategies aligned with company objectives aimed at creating mutually beneficial outcomes with key customers and suppliers.

Engineer Syed Amjad Iqbal is a highly qualified professional, with a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering (Telecommunication) and a Master's in Project Management from Sweden.
